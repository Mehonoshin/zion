### Tmux:
ctrl-a l   --- switch between two last windows
ctrl-a &   --- kill window
ctrl-a $   --- rename session
ctrl-a d   --- detach session
ctrl-a !   --- move pane to a new window
crtl-a l   --- switch to previous tab
